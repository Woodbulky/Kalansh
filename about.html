Our Story

From a family grocery shop in 1992 to empowering 90+ women in rural Maharashtra — this is the story of Kalansh.

Get to Know Us

A Taste of Heritage, Handcrafted with Love

Kalansh Udyog Nirmit is a women-powered organic food brand rooted in Kopargaon, Maharashtra. What began with a single craftsman and four women now brings together 90+ women artisans, 12 craftsmen, and a dedicated team — all making traditional sweets the way they were meant to be made.

Every Rajgira Ladoo, chikki, and jar of jaggery is crafted with pure amaranth, natural jaggery, and zero refined sugar or additives. It is heritage you can taste, made by hands that care.

The Journey

How Kalansh Began

1992

Nitin Manikchand Kale and Anil Manikchand Kale open a small grocery shop in Kopargaon. The shop offers spices, instant foods, and household essentials — the beginning of the Kale family's entrepreneurial journey.

2019

Rohit and Vinay Kale take over the family business. Inspired by the nutritional value of rajgira (amaranth) and the opportunity to create employment for local women, they launch production of Rajgira Ladoo and Peanut Chikki — starting in a small rented space in Kopargaon.

2020–2022

Products expand to include Til Ladoo, Murmura Ladoo, Peanut Crush Chikki, Rajgira Chikki, and Organic Jaggery. Distribution expands across Ahmadnagar, Nashik, and Pune districts. WhatsApp ordering begins.

2023

Company moves to a 10,000 sq ft industrial facility in Kopargaon. Team grows to 12 craftsmen, 90 women workers, 6 drivers, 2 accountants. Products listed on Flipkart, Amazon, and Jio Mart. ISO 22000:2018 certification achieved.

2024–Present

Presence in 20+ cities across Maharashtra. Expanding outside Maharashtra. Women's savings groups and additional stalls established. The mission continues — one ladoo at a time.

Our Founders

The People Behind Kalansh

Rohit Nitin Kale

Founder & CEO

Bachelor of Fine Arts (BFA), honored as Best Emerging Industrialist from Kopargaon. With a diverse background spanning McDonald's, painting, event setup, and film cameraman roles — Rohit's creative vision drives Kalansh's branding, advertising, and product quality. A social activist with deep community ties, he was inspired to create dignified employment for women in rural Maharashtra.

VK

Vinay Nitin Kale

Co-Founder

Brother of Rohit Kale, Vinay joined the venture from September 2019. He manages operations and distribution, ensuring that every product reaches customers on time and in perfect condition. Together, the brothers transformed a family grocery shop into a multifold business that now serves 20+ cities across Maharashtra.

Creating Opportunity

Additional small stalls are set up near residential areas so women can earn without long commutes. The Kalansh Women's Home Industry branch enables home-based work, and festival celebrations — Raksha Bandhan, Ganesh Utsav, Sankrant — are organised within the company.

Financial Empowerment

Kalansh Women's Savings Group provides financial empowerment beyond just wages — a mechanism for financial planning and long-term stability. Because empowerment is not just about a paycheck, it is about building a future.
