## Brand Story (Timeline)

### 1992
Nitin Manikchand Kale and Anil Manikchand Kale open a small grocery shop in Kopargaon. The shop offers spices, instant foods, and household essentials.

### 2019 — The Pivot
Rohit and Vinay Kale take over the family business. Inspired by the nutritional value of rajgira (amaranth) and the opportunity to create employment for local women, they launch production of Rajgira Ladoo and Peanut Chikki — starting in a small rented space in Kopargaon.

### 2020–2022 — Growth
Products expand to include Til Ladoo, Murmura Ladoo, Peanut Crush Chikki, Rajgira Chikki, and Organic Jaggery. Distribution expands across Ahmadnagar, Nashik, and Pune districts. WhatsApp ordering begins.

### 2023 — Scale
Company moves to a 10,000 sq ft industrial facility in Kopargaon. Team grows to 12 craftsmen, 90 women workers, 6 drivers, 2 accountants. Products listed on Flipkart, Amazon, and Jio Mart. ISO 22000:2018 certification achieved.

### 2024–Present — Expansion
Presence in 20+ cities across Maharashtra. Expanding outside Maharashtra. Women's savings groups and additional stalls established.

## Women's Empowerment (Feature this prominently)

Kalansh's core mission is creating dignified employment for women in rural Maharashtra who are unable to travel far from home for work.

- **90 women** work in production at the main facility
- Additional **small stalls** set up near residential areas so women can earn without long commutes
- Kalansh Women's Home Industry branch for home-based work
- Kalansh Women's Savings Group — financial empowerment beyond just wages
- Festival celebrations (Raksha Bandhan, Ganesh Utsav, Sankrant) organized within the company
