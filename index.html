Our Story

From a Family Grocery Shop to 90+ Women Empowered

In 1992, the Kale family opened a small grocery shop in Kopargaon. In 2019, brothers Rohit and Vinay Kale transformed it into something extraordinary — a mission to create organic, jaggery-based sweets while providing dignified employment to women in rural Maharashtra.

Today, Kalansh operates from a 10,000 sq ft ISO 22000:2018 certified facility with 110+ team members and products distributed across 20+ cities.

Why Kalansh

What Makes Us Different

100% Organic Ingredients

Every ingredient is sourced from verified organic farmers. No synthetic pesticides, no chemical fertilisers — just pure, natural food as it was meant to be.

Jaggery-Based, No Refined Sugar

We use pure organic jaggery instead of refined sugar. Rich in iron, minerals, and natural sweetness — the way Indian sweets were always made.

Women-Made with Care

90+ women artisans in Kopargaon handcraft every product. Each ladoo is rolled with pride, each chikki made with love — and every purchase supports rural women's empowerment.
