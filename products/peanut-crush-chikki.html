Peanut Crush Chikki

"All the flavour, smoother bite"

For those who love peanut chikki but prefer a finer, melt-in-mouth texture — our Peanut Crush Chikki is made with coarsely crushed roasted peanuts set in pure jaggery. The result is a smoother bite with all the nutty flavour of traditional chikki.

  • Pure jaggery base
  • High protein
  • No preservatives
