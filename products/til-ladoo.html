Til Ladoo

"A winter tradition, all year round"

Til Ladoo (Sesame Ladoo) is a beloved Indian sweet traditionally made during Makar Sankranti — but at Kalansh, we make it all year round. Toasted sesame seeds bound with pure organic jaggery and a touch of ghee. A small ladoo with a big nutritional punch: sesame is one of the richest sources of calcium and iron in the plant world.

Key Highlights

  • Rich in calcium and iron
  • Organic sesame seeds
  • Pure jaggery
  • Traditional Sankranti recipe
  • No refined sugar
IngredientsSesame Seeds (Til), Organic Jaggery, Ghee
Best ForWinter, Makar Sankranti, calcium-deficient diets, women's health
StorageStore in a cool dry place. Consume within 30 days.

The Women Behind Every Til Ladoo

Every Til Ladoo is hand-rolled by the women artisans of our Kopargaon facility — never by machine. Each ladoo is shaped warm, by hand, the traditional Maharashtrian way. When you choose Kalansh, you support 90+ women building independent livelihoods in rural Maharashtra.
